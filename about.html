About US

MenuPages is here to help you find online menus from your favorite restaurants nationwide.
You can search for restaurants and see their menus, prices, hours, locations, and more.

With 50,000+ restaurant menus in 1,000+ cities, MenuPages makes it easy to find exactly what you’re hungry for.
You can even order online for delivery or takeout for delicious, hassle-free dining.

MenuPages has been online since 2002 and in 2011, joined the Grubhub family of brands
that include Grubhub, Seamless, and EAT24.
